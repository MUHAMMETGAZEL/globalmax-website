About Us

GlobalMax is a hygiene product manufacturer based in Istanbul, Türkiye, committed to delivering quality, innovation, and professionalism since 2019.

We specialize in high-performance laundry perfumes (scent boosters) and dishwasher tablets, and have recently expanded our portfolio to include liquid laundry detergent.

Our guiding values—quality, innovation, flexibility, and professionalism—define every product we create and every partnership we build.

From product development to packaging, we ensure that every step reflects excellence and care.

Made in Türkiye, Delivered Worldwide

Strategically located in Istanbul, GlobalMax proudly manufactures all its products in Türkiye.

Our production capabilities are tailored to meet the demands of both local and international markets, allowing us to serve customers in Europe, the UK, America, and beyond.

We export our high-quality hygiene solutions worldwide, combining Turkish manufacturing strength with global hygiene standards.
